# ANALYTICAL FRAME
## Summary:
Amazon.com operates a diversified technology and commerce business organized into three segments: North America and International (encompassing online and physical retail, third-party seller services, subscription services, electronic devices, and advertising) and Amazon Web Services (AWS, providing cloud infrastructure and platform services). The company serves distinct constituencies—consumers, sellers, developers, enterprises, content creators, and advertisers—through digital marketplaces, physical retail locations, and on-demand cloud platforms. Revenue models span subscription (Prime), consumption-based (AWS), transaction and service fees (seller programs), advertising (ad services), and hardware sales (electronic devices).
