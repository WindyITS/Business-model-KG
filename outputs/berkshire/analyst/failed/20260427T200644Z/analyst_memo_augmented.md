# ANALYTICAL FRAME
## Summary:
Berkshire Hathaway is a decentralized holding company generating revenue through three major capital-intensive franchises—insurance (primary and reinsurance), freight rail transportation, and regulated utilities/energy—complemented by a diversified portfolio of autonomous manufacturing, service, and retailing subsidiaries. The business model emphasizes centralized capital allocation and investment management by the CEO, coupled with decentralized operational management of wholly-owned subsidiaries that maintain independent branding, distribution, and customer relationships.

## Explicit Support:
- "Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a holding company owning subsidiaries engaged in numerous diverse business activities... managed on an unusually decentralized basis."
- "The most important of these are insurance businesses... a freight rail transportation business and a group of utility and energy generation and distribution businesses."
- "Berkshire’s Chief Executive Officer is ultimately responsible for significant capital allocation decisions and investment activities."

# SEGMENTS
## Insurance
### Role in Business Model:
Core underwriting segment generating float through primary property/casualty and life/health insurance, plus global reinsurance of risks ceded by other insurers. Distinct sub-franchises operate as independently managed insurers with specialized customer targets, distribution methods, and regulatory statuses (admitted vs. non-admitted).
### Explicit Support:
- "Berkshire’s insurance subsidiaries provide insurance and reinsurance of property and casualty risks as well as life and health risks worldwide."
- "Berkshire’s insurance underwriting operations include the following groups: (1) GEICO, (2) Berkshire Hathaway Primary Group and (3) Berkshire Hathaway Reinsurance Group."
- "Nearly 90% of BH Primary net premiums written in 2025 were in the U.S., of which approximately 40% was written on a non-admitted basis."
### Customer Types:
- consumers | GEICO private passenger auto and related personal lines sold to individuals
- small businesses | Commercial lines through BH Direct and GUARD targeting SMBs; commercial auto and property coverage
- large enterprises | Reinsurance cedants (insurers/reinsurers); corporate primary coverage through specialty groups; railroads and utilities through specialty programs
- mid-market companies | Workers' compensation and commercial packages through BHHC and USLI
- healthcare organizations | Physicians, surgeons, dentists, hospitals, and senior care facilities through MedPro and MLMIC
- government agencies | Public entities through General Re Group specialty programs; military and postal uniform markets
### Channels:
- direct sales | GEICO direct response via telephone and internet; reinsurance treaties placed directly; structured settlement annuities
- online | GEICO internet-based application submission; biBERK.com and Threeinsurance.com platforms
- resellers | Insurance agents, wholesale brokers, managing general agents, and program administrators for primary commercial and specialty lines

Asset-heavy freight transportation infrastructure serving as a North American logistics backbone for manufacturing, agricultural, and resource industries, generating revenue through contractual and common carrier freight services across interconnected rail networks with trackage rights in the U.S., Canada, and Mexico.
### Explicit Support:
- "Burlington Northern Santa Fe, LLC... operates one of the largest freight rail transportation systems in North America."
- "transports a range of products and commodities derived from manufacturing, agricultural and natural resource industries."
- "BNSF Railway’s primary routes, including trackage rights, allow it to access major cities and certain ports in the western and southern U.S., as well as parts of Canada and Mexico."
### Customer Types:

- large enterprises | Agricultural and energy sector clients with volume shipping needs
### Channels:
- direct sales | Contractual agreements with shippers and common carrier published pricing
