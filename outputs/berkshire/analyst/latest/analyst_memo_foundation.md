# ANALYTICAL FRAME
## Summary:
Berkshire Hathaway is a decentralized holding company generating revenue through three dominant business anchors—insurance, freight rail transportation, and regulated utilities—supplemented by a diversified portfolio of manufacturing and service/retailing operations. The company operates with minimal centralized integration, emphasizing capital allocation and investment activities at the corporate level while granting operational autonomy to subsidiary businesses. Insurance operations generate significant "float" for investment while targeting underwriting profitability, BNSF provides contractual freight transportation across North America, and BHE operates regulated utility and pipeline monopolies alongside real estate services.

## Explicit Support:
- "Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a holding company owning subsidiaries engaged in numerous diverse business activities."
- "The most important of these are insurance businesses, conducted on both a primary basis and a reinsurance basis, a freight rail transportation business and a group of utility and energy generation and distribution businesses."
- "Berkshire's operating subsidiaries are managed on an unusually decentralized basis."

# SEGMENTS
## [Segment] Insurance
### Role in Business Model:
Underwrites property, casualty, life and health risks on a direct (primary) and reinsurance basis worldwide. Generates float (policyholder funds held for investment) while targeting underwriting profit over time through distinct operating groups with specialized risk selection and distribution methods.
### Explicit Support:
- "Berkshire's insurance business activities are conducted through numerous domestic and foreign-based insurance subsidiaries."
- "Berkshire's insurance subsidiaries provide insurance and reinsurance of property and casualty risks as well as life and health risks worldwide."
- "Berkshire expects to achieve an underwriting profit over time."
### Customer Types:
- consumers | GEICO sells private passenger automobile insurance directly to individuals
- large enterprises | Commercial property/casualty and reinsurance for corporate risk transfer
- small businesses | BH Direct and GUARD target small-to-medium commercial accounts
- healthcare organizations | MedPro provides healthcare liability insurance to physicians and facilities
### Channels:
- direct sales | GEICO's direct response marketing via telephone and Internet; reinsurance placed directly with ceding companies
- online | GEICO accepts applications and services policies via Internet
- resellers | Insurance agents, wholesale brokers, managing general agents for commercial and specialty lines
