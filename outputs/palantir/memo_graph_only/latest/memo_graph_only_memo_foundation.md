# ANALYTICAL FRAME
## Summary:
Palantir operates as a vertically integrated software platform company organized around two primary market segments: Government and Commercial. The company generates revenue through four principal software platforms—Gotham (defense and intelligence operations), Foundry (enterprise data operating system), Apollo (cloud-agnostic delivery infrastructure), and AIP (AI integration layer)—which collectively enable large institutions to integrate data, decisions, and operations at scale. The business model targets complex, large-scale deployments through a direct sales force, with recent expansion into developer-access tiers to broaden market reach.

## Explicit Support:
- "We have built four principal software platforms, Palantir Gotham (“Gotham”), Palantir Foundry (“Foundry”), Palantir Apollo (“Apollo”), and Palantir Artificial Intelligence Platform (“AIP”)."
- "Of the $2.9 billion in revenue that we generated in 2024, 55% came from customers in the government segment, and 45% came from customers in the commercial segment."
- "Our business continues to have a global presence. In 2024, we earned 66% of our revenue from customers in the United States, and 34% from those abroad."
- "We have invested and may continue to invest in an account-based sales force to identify and capture new customers and opportunities."
